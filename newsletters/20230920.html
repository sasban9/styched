COMPANY ONE LINER:

We are Styched, a global D2C brand, and we make fashion affordable by creating new and evolving styles every week at a quarter of the price compared to any other brand owing to our patented Production On Demand tech and Zero inventory Zero warehouse model.

ACHIEVEMENTS/LESSONS LEARNED

1. Zymrat Acquisition - We completed Zymrat acquisition last week. Currently, we are working towards liquidating the left over inventory before we launch new products and styles.

2. Flatheads Pre-Ordering - We are planning to start Pre-Ordering of Flatheads 2.0 on 20th October, and start shipping from 1 November 2023. For Flatheads, we are starting a semi on-demand model, with inventory on Soles and Uppers. Printing and Assembly will happen on demand.

3. Myntra relaunch - We worked on a more viable commercial model with the Myntra team so that we can get to profitability faster. We would now work on a 4% commission model instead of the earlier 14% commission model.

4. Styched PLUS - We are launching Styched Plus in UAE, which is a semi premium, bespoke yet affordable brand with more customisation options in every apparel. Styles would be priced between 90 AED to 399 AED.

5. Acquisitions - We are evaluating the acquisition of EShakti.com, a zero inventory customised apparel brand with large market penetration in the USA. We are working with SOSV and mentors to create a workable model.

6. First Franchisee Hub in Tiruppur going live on 1st October. Capacity of ~100 orders per day initially.

7. Event - We would be present in the Fashion Tech Week 2023 on 5th October. I am a speaker at one of the panel discussions. The session "On-Demand Manufacturing and India: Feasibility and Opportunities" will take place on 5th October post lunch.
